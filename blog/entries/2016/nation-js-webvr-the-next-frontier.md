# NationJS WebVR: The Next Frontier

	Abstract: As new virtual reality headsets are quickly becoming available to the consumer, WebVR is opening up new frontiers of interaction for the web. Beyond gaming and entertainment, WebVR is creating whole new classes of immersive applications for the Web Platform in communication, education, shopping, and more.
